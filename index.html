About Us

Welcome to the Shopping List Generator!

This application helps you organize your shopping needs efficiently. You can create lists, manage items, and even track your purchase habits.

How to use:

  • Create a list: Enter a title and items in the input box on the home page.
  • Manage lists: Check off items as you buy them. You can also delete items or entire lists.
  • Focus Mode: Click the expand icon on a list to view it in full screen.
  • Backup: Download and upload backups of your data to keep it safe.
